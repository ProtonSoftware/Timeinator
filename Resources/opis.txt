+ANGLAIS
Easy and fast leisure time managing.
------------------------------------
For whom has this app been created?
If you are ambitious and have a lot of tasks to accomplish and you always forget something, then you are just like us and this app is made for you.

How does it work?
Simply enter any task you want to do and configure it using wide variety of options.
Next pick few tasks that you are interested to start at the moment. You can use convenient filtering options to navigate on your big task list.
Set time that you can spend before going back to your duties, so the app can calculate time for each of the selected tasks. It will ask whether yoou want to reorder some task or use default ordering. When time runs out you will be notified and asked if you need a break or go straight to your next assignment.
Our app will make sure that all of your tasks are started in desired time.
It is our priority to have all assignments fitted in specified time, what means that the longer breaks you take the shorter the duration of your tasks.

This tool has been created as a solution to manage time in a way that allows you to work on a project, watch a soap opera and read a book in an effective and controlled manner.
