+ANGLAIS
Fully exploit your free time.
------------------------------------
For whom has this app been created?
If you are ambitious and have a lot of tasks to accomplish and you always forget something, then you are just like us and this app is made for you.

When creating this app, we followed these two ideas:
- Our mind is more creative if we give a rest from long, repetetive tasks - that's why our app reminds you to change your current job, so then you get better performance and can push yourself to the limits.
- In order to achieve great things, all you need is to repeat any task systematically, whether it is learning a language, reading a thick book or anything else. Do it until you reach your dreamed goal - that is why we allow you to set long term objectives, it is an universal and most efficient way of self improvment.

How does it work?
Our app calculates when you should change your current assignment to have time for the rest of them. Thanks to that you can fully use your potential every day.

Step by step
Set yourself time limit when you have to go back to work, then our app calculates duration of every task you have picked in such way that they fit in specified time range. You will be offered to change the order of tasks and then you can start. As time for a task runs out you will hear an alarm telling you to proceed to the next task, you can take a break now if you wish. Application takes care that you finish doing everything at specified time but this can be disabled in settings so you can set the target amount of time per task instead.
