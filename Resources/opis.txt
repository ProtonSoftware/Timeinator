+ANGLAIS
Fully exploit your free time.
------------------------------------
For whom has this app been created?
If you are ambitious and have a lot of tasks to accomplish and you always forget something, then you are just like us and this app is made for you.

When creating this app, we followed these two ideas:
- Our mind is more creative if we give a rest from long, repetetive tasks - that's why our app reminds you to change your current job, so then you get better performance and can push your brain to the limits.
- In order to achieve great things, all you need is to repeat any task systematically, whether it is learning a language, reading a thick book or anything else. Do it until you reach your dreamed goal - that is why we allow you to set long term objectives, it is an universal and most efficient way of self improvment.

How does it work?
Our app calculates when you should change your current assignment to have time for the rest of them. Thanks to that you can fully use your potential.

Step by step
Simply enter any task you want to do and configure it using wide variety of options.
Next pick few tasks that you are interested to start at the moment. You can use convenient filtering options to navigate on your big task list.
Set time that you can spend before going back to your duties, so the app can calculate time for each of the selected tasks. It will ask whether you want to reorder some task or use default ordering. When time runs out you will be notified and asked if you need a break or go straight to your next assignment.
Our app will make sure that all of your tasks are fit in desired time, unless you change settings to mark all jobs as finished only after you spend the exact amount of time on them without counting breaks.
